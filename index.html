WELCOME

Dehradun Public School, popularly known as DDPS, is a co-educational, private school in Uttar Pradesh, India. Founded in 1983, DDPS is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and caters to students from Pre-School to Class XII. It is one of the city's largest schools with a student strength of over 9000+ excellent academic as well as outstanding co-curricular performance of its students year on year. Our beautiful, spacious, state-of-the-art, and lush green campuses are spread out in Ghaziabad and Muzaffarnagar. We strive to continue growing so t hat we can bring the DDPS experience to even more families across the city.
